A little bit about me.

I am Jesi. I’m a front end developer and web designer, and Code for Brunch is my studio. I can’t wait to...
… translate your ideas and goals into an out-of-the-box web design, or a complete website.
… seamlessly take over the coding work so that you could fully focus on what you love doing best, designing.
… be a part of your awesome team and build brands or tackle design projects together!
… help your brand with a re-design or feature upgrade so that you could increase your customer base.

Some days I wrangle JavaScript, some days I’m prototyping with Adobe XD, and on yet other days I’m working on offline pet projects - such as a van conversion (yep, self-isolating somewhere with more nature than people sounds like a great idea). One thing is constant: I would look up from my work, and it’s always time for brunch!
Last April 2020, I got into coding and design as my way of giving the global pandemic my middle finger. The world was a dumpster fire and enrolling into one of Skillcrush’s blueprints was the best decision I made for my family. Had COVID-19 not happened, would I still have gotten into coding or design? Yes. If not for anything else, tech has been helping me parent better! Furthermore, my roles have constantly engaged me in projects that really matter, and in advocacies I sincerely care about.

Hand-Coded HTML & CSS

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 and lean, fast-loading CSS by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, to collaborate on and to troubleshoot. I also make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Mobile First Approach

I pride myself on designing websites mobile first by understanding what the user needs and creatively adapting the capabilities of mobile phones to meet those needs.

Trendsetting Web Design

You can trust me to take your ideas & quickly, accurately convert them into a design that has user experience in mind & owning trends which work with your goals.
